## What is a Model?
A model represents a system you want to understand. Models can be:
- Diagrams, flowcharts
- Simulations, computer models
- Mathematical equations
- Physical recreations or prototypes

Models help you:
- Focus on specific parts of your topic
- Make predictions
- Communicate ideas
- Collect or visualize data
